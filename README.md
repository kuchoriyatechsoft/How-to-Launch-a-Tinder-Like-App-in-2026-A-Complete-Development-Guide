## Can I build a Tinder clone app without coding knowledge?

## What does the development roadmap look like?

7. Ongoing Maintenance

---

## How long does AI matchmaking take to develop?

Basic recommendation engines can be built within a few weeks.

Advanced AI models usually require **4–8 additional weeks** plus sufficient user data for training.

---

## Should AI compatibility scoring be included in the MVP?

Not necessarily.

Many startups launch with rule-based matching first and introduce AI recommendations after collecting enough user behavior data.

---

## Should I choose a clone script or custom development?

Choose a **clone script** if your goal is quick validation.

Choose **custom development** if you plan to build a scalable, feature-rich platform with complete ownership.
